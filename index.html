Welcome to Super Car Share!

Do you love using car share services, but don't always find that their apps are very user-friendly? Me too, which is why I made this app.

This app currently only works with the Evo Car Share service in Vancouver, BC, Canada. If you want to use a different service - sorry - it's not supported!
